Courgette Linguine

Ingredients

  • Courgettes x 2
  • Linguine
  • Garlic clove x 1
  • Chilli flakes
  • Lemons x 2
  • Pecorino
  • Fresh mint or basil

Method

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Sautee courgette rounds in plenty of oil
  3. Add crushed garlic and chilli flakes
  4. Stir through pasta along with lemon juice, zest, grated pecorino, and chopped herbs
  5. Add more olive oil and season well
